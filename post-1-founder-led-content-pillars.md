# Post Type — Founder-Led Content: 3 Pillars
**Author:** Grant Lee (Co-founder & CEO, Gamma)
**Source:** Reconstructed from YouTube interview with Lara Acosta
**Content Type:** Educational / milestone announcement hybrid

## Observed Content Pattern

Grant posts approximately 5x/week on LinkedIn. All posts fall into one of three buckets:

### Pillar 1: Education
- Topics: visual communication, storytelling, copywriting, design, brand consistency
- Format: frameworks, step-by-step breakdowns, "what I've learned" lists
- Example angle: "How do you write good copy for a deck?" → breaks into hook, body, CTA

### Pillar 2: Founder Story / Build in Public
- Topics: Gamma's journey (2022 beta → March 2023 relaunch → 70M users), fundraising struggles, team milestones
- Format: narrative arc (low → high), uses others' stories first (e.g., Morris Chang / TSMC) before inserting personal reflection
- Key technique: "Halo effect" — tell a known person's story, draw the parallel to your own. Reduces "cringe" and builds authority faster.

### Pillar 3: Announcements & Milestones
- Topics: product launches, revenue milestones, partnerships, customer wins, recruiting
- Format: calendar-mapped, coordinated with marketing team
- Ranging from large (annual product launches) to small (partnership announcements, customer case studies)

## Hook Philosophy
- Every post passes the "would someone share this or bookmark it?" test before publishing
- Hook must be a scroll-stopper; body must make the takeaway shareable
- LinkedIn and X tested simultaneously early on → LinkedIn won → doubled down

## Key Metrics (shared in interview)
- LinkedIn accounts for 25%+ of cross-platform Gamma signups
- LinkedIn also drives recruiting spikes and B2B partnership DMs
- Organic-first from 2022–present; influencer partnerships layered on top only after word-of-mouth was confirmed

## Psychological Triggers Used
- **Halo effect**: Borrowing authority from well-known figures (Morris Chang) before asserting personal credibility
- **Build in public**: Vulnerability + aspiration — shares the failures alongside milestones
- **Scarcity of insight**: "Things I wish I knew starting Gamma" framing targets late-career entrepreneurs

## B2B SaaS Applications
- Map a content calendar with 1 educational post + 1 story/journey post + 1 milestone post per week
- Use "halo borrowing" for early-stage founders who don't yet have big milestones to share
- LinkedIn = top-of-funnel for both customers AND recruits AND partners (track all three, not just MQL)
