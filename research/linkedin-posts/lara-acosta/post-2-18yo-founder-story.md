# Post 2 — "I just spoke to an 18-year-old making 6-figures"
**Author:** Lara Acosta
**Date:** ~1 week ago
**Type:** Storytelling / hook-framework post

## Content
I just spoke to an 18-year-old making 6-figures. EIGHTEEN!

Here's how I'd write his first LinkedIn post — focus on 3 key things:
1. The storyline ("I'm 18")
2. The proof ("I run a successful business")
3. The education ("Here's how I did it")

Example structure:
"I turned 18 last week, my business hit $100k. Built my business from my childhood bedroom. Started at 15 selling sweets at school. By 17 I'd gotten into online business. By 18 I hit my first $100k with an agency."

Closing insight: "Every story is a way to stand out your competitors can't steal. Use it — in the hook, in the middle, in the end. There's 100 ways to tell the same story. You just need to start by doing it once."
