## Content
"Repeat yourself, please. This is how you grow a following. This is how you become a thought leader. This is how you stand out in a saturated niche. Own a niche by talking about it every single time."

Weekly content routine:
1. Pick the topic you want to be known for
2. Find 3-5 subtopics that tie into the main topic
3. Pick 1 subtopic to talk about for 4 days of the week
4. Use different angles (education or storytelling)
5. Repeat the following week

Claim: "This is simple, but it's what's built me a $2M+ business. Repetition builds reputation over time."
