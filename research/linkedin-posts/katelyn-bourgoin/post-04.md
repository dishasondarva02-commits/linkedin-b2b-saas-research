**Hook type:** Direct question open — minimal, no story, no data. Pure statement of desire.

**Engagement driver:** "Trust = sales" reduces a complex B2B concept to an equation. Equations feel certain. Certainty feels credible.
**Behavioral economics principle:** Cognitive ease — shorter posts require less effort to process and are perceived as more insightful. Mere exposure effect — short posts like this build familiarity and brand recall at low cognitive cost.
**17-intent mapping:** Aspiration Reinforcement Intent — confirms what the reader already suspects is true, validating their worldview
