# Intent Map — LinkedIn B2B SaaS Content
**Framework:** Disha Sondarva's 17-Intent Ontology × Katelyn Bourgoin's JTBD × Peep Laja's Message-Market Fit
**Purpose:** Map collected expert content to buying intent layers to build a content calendar that covers the full buyer journey

---

## The 3-Layer Annotation System

Every post collected in this research is annotated across three layers:

```
LAYER 1: Psychological Trigger (Katelyn Bourgoin)
    → What unconscious mechanism makes the reader stop and feel something?

LAYER 2: Buying Intent (17-Intent Ontology)
    → Where in the buying journey does this post live?

LAYER 3: Clarity Score (Peep Laja)
    → Does the message land in 3 seconds? Scale 1–5.
```

---

## Intent Layer Definitions

| Intent | Definition | Buyer state | B2B SaaS content type |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1. Problem Unawareness** | Buyer doesn't know the problem exists | "I wing it and it's fine" | Named problem posts |
| **2. Problem Recognition** | Buyer senses something is wrong | "This feels harder than it should" | Agitation posts, trigger event stories |
| **3. Problem Articulation** | Buyer can now name the problem | "We're losing candidates to disorganization" | Framework posts, "there's a name for this" |
| **4. Solution Awareness** | Buyer knows a solution category exists | "There are tools for this" | Category education posts |
| **5. Solution Consideration** | Buyer is evaluating options | "Which tool is right for us?" | Feature-benefit comparison posts |
| **6. Solution Validation** | Buyer wants proof before deciding | "Show me it works for teams like mine" | Customer story, before/after posts |
| **7. Decision** | Buyer is ready to buy | "How do I start?" | Trial CTA, demo, pricing posts |
| **8. Onboarding** | Buyer just started using | "How do I get value fast?" | Quick-win tip posts, setup guides |
| **9. Loyalty Reinforcement** | Current user looking for validation | "Did I make the right choice?" | Power user story, feature deep-dive |
| **10. Advocacy Activation** | Happy user who could refer others | "I want to share this" | Shareable insight posts |
| **11. Identity Aspiration** | Buyer wants to be a certain kind of person | "I want to be the founder who hires well" | Identity-framed posts |
| **12. Social Proof Seeking** | Buyer needs peer validation | "Who else uses this?" | Community/testimonial posts |
| **13. Fear Activation** | Buyer is motivated by loss aversion | "What am I risking by NOT doing this?" | Cost-of-inaction posts |
| **14. Curiosity** | Buyer is passively interested | "Hm, interesting" | Hook posts, data posts, contrarian takes |
| **15. Relatability** | Buyer feels seen and understood | "This is exactly my situation" | Humor, POV posts, day-in-the-life |
| **16. Authority Recognition** | Buyer assessing if you know their world | "Do they understand my world?" | Thought leadership, industry insight |
| **17. Trust Building** | Buyer accumulating evidence over time | "I keep seeing them. They seem legit." | Consistent presence, mere exposure |

## Weekly Intent Distribution Target (B2B SaaS)

```
MONDAY:    Intent 16 (Authority) — thought leadership
TUESDAY:   Intent 2-3 (Problem) — agitation and naming
WEDNESDAY: Intent 15 (Relatability) — humor, POV
THURSDAY:  Intent 5-6 (Consideration) — feature-benefit, proof
FRIDAY:    Intent 11-17 (Aspiration + Trust) — identity or short insight
```

This ensures that a new follower who discovers your brand on any day encounters content that matches where they are in their buying journey.
