## Core Definition

**Dark social** refers to the untrackable, word-of-mouth channels where B2B buying actually happens — Slack DMs, private LinkedIn messages, podcast mentions, WhatsApp groups, email forwards, and hallway conversations.

These channels are "dark" because standard attribution software (Google Analytics, HubSpot last-touch) cannot see them.

---

## The Measurement Gap

Chris Walker identified a parallel concept: the **Measurement Gap** — the maddening disconnect between where attribution software says leads come from (paid search, direct traffic) and where buyers actually discovered you (a LinkedIn post their colleague forwarded, a podcast episode, a Slack recommendation).

**Before naming:** "We're getting leads but can't scale. Our attribution says Google Ads works but it doesn't feel right."
**After naming:** "We have a Measurement Gap. We need to invest in dark social channels."

---

## Why It Matters for LinkedIn B2B SaaS

1. **LinkedIn IS a dark social channel** — Most LinkedIn consumption happens in-feed without clicks. Shares happen via DM, not public repost.
2. **Attribution lies** — If you only measure link clicks, you'll underinvest in LinkedIn and overinvest in paid search capture.
3. **Content must be forwardable** — Zero-click posts that deliver complete value are designed to be copied into Slack threads and forwarded via DM.

## Walker's Demand Creation Model (Related)

| Type | Definition | LinkedIn role |
|---|---|---|
| **Demand capture** | Intercepting buyers actively searching | Not LinkedIn's strength |
| **Demand creation** | Building awareness before buyers know they're buying | LinkedIn's primary job |

LinkedIn organic = demand creation. Pair with SEO/paid for demand capture.

---

## Psychological Mechanisms

| Mechanism | Dark social connection |
|---|---|
| **Social proof** | Peer recommendations in private channels carry more weight than ads |
| **Trust transfer** | When a colleague forwards your LinkedIn post in Slack, their trust transfers to you |
| **Mere exposure** | Multiple dark social touchpoints before a buyer ever visits your website |

---

## B2B SaaS Application

**Track dark social signals instead of (or alongside) clicks:**
- "I saw your LinkedIn post" in demo calls
- Inbound DMs referencing specific posts
- Email replies mentioning content
- "A colleague shared this with me" on signup forms

**Content designed for dark social:**
- Self-contained insights (zero-click)
- Named frameworks people can reference in conversation ("Have you heard of Spreadsheet Hiring Syndrome?")
- Contrarian takes worth debating in Slack threads
