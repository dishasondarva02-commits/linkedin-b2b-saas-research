## Core Frameworks

### 1. Post Anatomy
```
Line 1:   HOOK — stop the scroll
Lines 2-4: AGITATION — make the problem felt
Lines 5-10: VALUE — actionable insight
Line 11:  PIVOT — reframe or revelation
Final:    CTA — engagement prompt (never a link)
```

### 2. Strategic Commenting
- Comment on 10–20 target accounts daily before posting
- Comments must add value, not "Great post!"
- Build relationships with accounts whose audiences overlap your ICP
- Early engagement on your own posts within 30 minutes boosts distribution

### 3. Engagement Systems
- Reply to every comment on your posts
- Use questions as CTAs to drive comment volume
- "3+1 comment" technique: add follow-up comments to your own post to spark conversation

## Psychological Triggers

- **Loss aversion:** Agitation section makes the cost of inaction felt
- **Authority:** Precise copywriting demonstrates expertise before stating it
- **Reciprocity:** Giving full value in the post earns engagement
- **Social proof:** Engagement velocity signals quality to the algorithm

---

## B2B SaaS Application

- Run every post through the anatomy checklist before publishing
- Build a list of 30 ICP accounts; comment daily for 30 days before expecting reach
- Never open with "Excited to announce" — always hook first
- CTAs should prompt comments ("What's your biggest hiring bottleneck?") not clicks
