## Core Frameworks

### 1. Founder-Led Content Model
- The founder/CEO is the most credible voice in B2B
- Corporate voice kills engagement; human voice builds trust
- LinkedIn posts should sound like a person talking to a person, not a press release

### 2. Exit Five Community Flywheel
```
LinkedIn content (awareness)
  → Newsletter (trust)
  → Exit Five Live sessions (depth)
  → Paid community (monetization)
  → Member-generated content (social proof loop)
```

### 3. "Nobody Wants to Read Your Marketing"
- Operate from inbox empathy — assume you're competing with 1,000 other emails
- Give value before asking for anything
- Would YOU take time to read this email/post?

### 4. B2B Brand Without Being Corporate
- Share opinions, not just facts
- Feature other voices (counterintuitive trust builder)
- Reply to comments and newsletter replies — prove a human is there

### 5. Email as Owned Audience
- LinkedIn builds familiarity; email builds owned relationship
- Reply rate > open rate as trust signal
- Weekly cadence creates "air cover" marketing machine
