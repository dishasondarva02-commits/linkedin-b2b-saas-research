## Core Frameworks

### 1. Create Once, Distribute Forever (CODF)
- Creation = 20% of effort
- Distribution = 80% of effort
- One deep asset → repurposed across every channel

### 2. Content Distribution Stack
```
CREATE: Research → Write → Design
DISTRIBUTE: LinkedIn → Email → Twitter/X → YouTube → Podcast → Community
MEASURE: Dark social signals + engagement quality
ITERATE: Double down on what gets forwarded, not just liked
```

### 3. Research-as-Marketing
- Publish original research that becomes industry reference material
- Data-driven posts earn backlinks, shares, and authority simultaneously
- Example: Foundation's annual content marketing reports

---

## Psychological Triggers

- **Authority:** Original research positions you as the source, not a commentator
- **Social proof:** Client logos and case study metrics
- **Cognitive ease:** Frameworks with memorable names (CODF) become shareable shorthand

---

## B2B SaaS Application

- Write one quarterly "State of [Your Category]" research piece → 20+ LinkedIn posts
- Every product update → 5 distribution formats minimum
- Track which posts get forwarded in Slack (dark social) not just which get likes
- Build a repurposing checklist: every newsletter issue → 3 LinkedIn posts + 1 email + 1 carousel
