## Core Frameworks

### 1. Message-Market Fit
- Before scaling content, test whether messages land with actual buyers
- Score messaging on: clarity, relevance, differentiation, intent to demo
- Most B2B content fails because of confusion, not because the idea is wrong

### 2. The 3-Second Clarity Test
Can a stranger understand your message in 3 seconds?
- Score 1: Confusing — reader has no idea what you do or why it matters
- Score 3: Clear but generic — understood but not compelling
- Score 5: Crystal clear + compelling — instant understanding + desire to learn more

### 3. Jargon Stripping
- Replace insider language with buyer language
- Test: would your ICP use this word in a conversation with a colleague?
- If not, cut it

---

## Psychological Triggers

- **Cognitive ease:** Clear messages feel true (fluency heuristic)
- **Loss aversion:** Confusion = dropout. Every unclear word loses readers.
- **Authority:** Evidence-based testing > opinion-based copywriting

---

## B2B SaaS Application

- Score every LinkedIn post 1–5 on clarity before publishing
- Run Wynter-style testing: show post hooks to 5 ICP contacts, ask "what does this company do?"
- If they can't answer in one sentence, rewrite the hook
- Apply to homepage copy AND LinkedIn posts — same clarity standard
